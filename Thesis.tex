\abstract{\addtocontents{toc}{\vspace{1em}} % Add a gap in the Contents, for aesthetics
The \CTC algorithm performs variably on different corpora, and research has suggested that better algorithm designs are needed. The hassle of manually tuning the algorithm design for each corpus necessitate some form of automatic optimisation of the \CTC algorithm's design. This thesis describes a genetic algorithm that automatically produces optimised algorithm designs for the \CTC algorithm, and demonstrates the feasibility of this algorithm in terms of effectiveness, computing time, equipment requirements, and knowledge.
}
